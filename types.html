Types of Foster Care

  • Kinship (or relative): Children will be cared for by a family member, either on an informal, voluntary or formal basis. Informal would not require the inolvement of child protective services. Conversely, in a formal kinship, the state has custody and places the child with a relative. Voluntary involves child protecive services but not to the extent of a formal placement.
